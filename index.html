FAQ

What is MacUpdate Desktop?

A centralized tool to discover, install, update, launch, and uninstall macOS apps efficiently.

How do I update an app?

Select it in the list. If available, click the Update button in the details panel.

How do I launch or stop an app?

Select the app. Use Launch to open or Stop if running.

How do I uninstall?

Select the app, click Uninstall, and choose to remove data if desired.

What do icons mean?

Blue triangle: app running. Exclamation: update available.

Where are settings?

Top-right corner. Manage license, Daemon Helper, and software updates there.
